About

CNC Machining Services is an outsourcing company that involves the construction of whole products or, in some instances, a single element of a larger product. Precision machining services include boring, drilling, hexing, mill polishing, slotting and threading. Our location is in Houston, TX, where it has been since it started back in 2013 with only one machine. Since then, we have grown considerably to a 4,000 sq. ft. location with broader capabilities and equipment such as:

  • CNC lathe machines
  • CNC mill
  • Manual mill
  • Manual lathe
  • Automatic saw
Our expert care in safety and quality puts us head and shoulders above our competition. We believe in high-quality work and timely deliveries. Within our shops, we value safety, integrity and immense dedication to our customers around the clock. We cater primarily to the aerospace, automotive and oil and gas sectors of the community with our services. We operate with multiple facets of the community, as well, performing our business in both English and Spanish. We take immense pride in our products, services and employees and strive to achieve our goals for the greater good.
